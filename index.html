Shop ID Contacts

This tool requires the SDP-PII permit to access staff contact data. Without it, queries will fail with a 403 error. The permit is valid for 8 hours — after it expires you'll need to reapply. Apply for the SDP-PII permit →
Enter one or more Storefront IDs to look up shop contacts. Up to 5 shops can be searched at once — separate them with commas or put each on a new line. Click Look up or press ⌘ Return to start. Results appear in tabs when multiple shops are entered.
